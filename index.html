About Alien

Movies in the series

There are multiple movies within the Alien franchise, prequels and sequels alike. The first movie was released in 1979 and was very popular during the 80s. It was so popular that they director released 5 sequels/prequels with it. The main antagonist within these movies is a big alien known as the "Xenomorph", it became a classic figure within the horror genre.

Throughout the years game companies even released a few games about the Alien franchise. The movies won 8 awards in total, varying from the "Academy Award for Best Visual Effects" up to "BAFTA Award for Best Sound" and "Saturn Award for Best Science Fiction Film". The movies had great sound design, which gave the audience a very eerie athmosphere, which in turn made the movies and characters more popular.

Here is a list of the movies starting from the first one, making our way to the newest one.

  1. Alien (1979)
  2. Aliens(1986)
  3. Aliens(1992)
  4. Alien Resurrection(1997)
  5. Prometheus(2012)
  6. Alien Covenant(2017)
